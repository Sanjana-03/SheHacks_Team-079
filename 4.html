Home decor Curtains

Everyone has their own choice of selecting different curtain designs.

1. Pencil Pleat Curtain



It is pencil pleat latest curtain design for living room. At top creative pleats are given to curtain cloth. There are black colour layers carved out on curtain which is wide at someplace while closing at some other place. Layers on plain cloth look extremely stunning.

2. Eyelet Curtain



This beautiful curtain appears out to be elegant and sophisticated. There are coloured lining at top of curtain and rest is pure plain white. The cushion kept on the chair is also getting matched with curtain design. Half of the curtain is tied up with a broad white belt.

3. Sheer Curtain




This is grey and white modern curtain design which is light in fabric. Two extra curtains are kept over the rod with the help of which downfall layer is given on window. In between, there is a plain white curtain which is accompanied by a plain grey curtain on both sides.

4. Lace Overlay Curtain



This curtain design idea is a pleasing one. It is plain inside and outside, there is white lace fabric attached over it. There is a floral pattern on this lace on the upside. This curtain pattern depicts the knowledge of fashion and enlightens those who view it.

5. Embroided Curtain



This new style curtain is having stupefying embroidery work. The whole white curtain cloth contains floral embroidery print of the same colour. This long sober curtain is having a startling look. It is purely delightful having a sublime quality and lovely appearance.
