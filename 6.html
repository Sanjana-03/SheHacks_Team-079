Hand Made indian designer dresses

Indian wear is one of the most exquisite and intricate attire that you will find around the world. The garments that are worn are normally adorned with beads and sequins and are embroidered too. This brings out the beauty of these garments. The Indian wear consists of salwar kameez, lehenga, saree, etc. Each of these beautiful dresses is perfect for the Indian girl or woman. Make some of these your choices too and see how beautiful you will look in them. The Indian dress scene is now making major trends in the fashion industry. So check out these gorgeous Indian dresses, which we have listed here and choose the best.

1. Jacket Style Kurta Set



One of the Indian bridal dresses also is the most chosen one. This jacket style kurta is perfect for that memorable day. The kurta is made of two layers. The inner layer is plain with stunning colour. The outer jacket part is embroidered to perfection. The long sleeves have the embroidery pattern on the sleeve ends.

2. Layered Kurta



A new twist to the traditional Anarkali is the layered Anarkali suit. This dress has an Indian style dress look, but the lower half is not one piece. The suit’s bottom is layered in three layers, and each layer is edged with a golden border. This gives it a rich look and is thus perfect for special occasions like weddings, engagements, etc.

3. Zardosi Lehenga




Zardosi work is another really cool traditional Indian embroidery technique that adds glamour to any garment. This stunning Zardosi set is made modern with the use of embroidered belt at the waistline. The lehenga choli is perfect as is, but the belt changes it to another level. You can add a dupatta that is held in place with the belt.

4. Off Shoulder Lehenga Suit



Among the long Indian dresses style is this off shoulder lehenga suit. The choli has been stitched with the off-shoulder design. It has sleeves up to the wrists. The lehenga is pretty with embroidery work that is focused mainly on the bottom. A dupatta with the set also has the same work on it to add to the charm.

5. Georgette Gown



Georgette is a great material to use for Indian garments. This long Indian dress is made dazzling with the use of georgette material. The embroidery work done on the long dress is perfect and stunning. This is special for occasions like ceremonies, functions, festivals, etc.
